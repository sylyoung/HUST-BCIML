**Axis:** Transfer / adaptation strategy &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Paradigm:** MI &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Dataset:** BNCI2014001 (cross-subject LOSO)

## Mechanism
Margin Disparity Discrepancy bounds the target error by a margin-based disparity between the main classifier and an adversarial auxiliary classifier, minimized through a gradient-reversal step. It has the tightest across-seed variance in the whole sweep.
