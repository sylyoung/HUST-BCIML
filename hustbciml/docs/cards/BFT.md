# BFT

**Axis:** Transfer / adaptation strategy &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Paradigm:** MI &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Dataset:** BNCI2014001 (cross-subject LOSO)

**Stage configuration:** `EA · no-aug · EEGNet · Linear · BFT (test-time, no gradient)`

## Mechanism
Backpropagation-Free Transformations (the BFT-A variant) pass each trial through K label-preserving transforms at test time and aggregate the predictions with a temperature-sharpened weighted softmax, with no gradient step. It adapts by test-time-augmentation aggregation rather than by updating parameters.
