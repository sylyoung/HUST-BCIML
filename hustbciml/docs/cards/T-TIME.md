# T-TIME

**Axis:** Transfer / adaptation strategy &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Paradigm:** MI &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Dataset:** BNCI2014001 (cross-subject LOSO)

**Stage configuration:** `online-EA · no-aug · EEGNet · Linear · TTIME`

## Mechanism
Test-Time Information Maximization for online motor imagery: the source-trained model adapts on the streaming target by minimizing prediction entropy while maximizing marginal-class diversity (an information-maximization loss), with online incremental Euclidean Alignment updating the reference as each trial arrives. It has the tightest across-seed variance of the strong strategies; a 5-seed prediction ensemble lifts it further (see the Multi-seed ensemble section of RESULTS.md).

## Provenance
- **Paper / source:** Li et al. IEEE TBME (T-TIME); online test-time information maximization
- **Implementation:** Refactored from DeepTransferEEG (the T-TIME paper's own code, same author) onto the tta-mode Strategy ABC; no third-party code.
