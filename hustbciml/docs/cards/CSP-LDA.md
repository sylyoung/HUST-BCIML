# CSP-LDA

**Axis:** Classical (network-free) &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Paradigm:** MI &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Dataset:** BNCI2014001 (cross-subject LOSO)

**Stage configuration:** `EA · CSP spatial filters · (no backbone) · LDA · fit`

## Mechanism
Common Spatial Patterns plus Linear Discriminant Analysis. CSP learns spatial filters that maximize the band-power variance ratio between the two motor-imagery classes on the EA-aligned trials; the log-variance features feed an LDA classifier. The classic MI pipeline — network-free and deterministic — and it beats the deep EA-EEGNet reference on this dataset.

## Provenance
- **Paper / source:** Ramoser et al. 2000 (CSP) + LDA; Blankertz et al. 2008 CSP tutorial
- **Implementation:** CSP from MNE and LDA from scikit-learn on the fit-mode Strategy and Head ABCs; both are standard BSD-3 libraries.
