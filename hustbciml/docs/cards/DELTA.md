# DELTA

**Axis:** Transfer / adaptation strategy &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Paradigm:** MI &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Dataset:** BNCI2014001 (cross-subject LOSO)

**Stage configuration:** `EA · no-aug · EEGNet · Linear · DELTA`

## Mechanism
DELTA performs entropy minimization with a class-imbalance-corrected diversity term (dynamic online reweighting of classes), which prevents the collapse to majority-class predictions that degrades naive test-time adaptation.
