# EA-EEGConformer

**Axis:** Network (backbone) &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Paradigm:** MI &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Dataset:** BNCI2014001 (cross-subject LOSO)

**Stage configuration:** `EA · no-aug · EEGConformer · Linear · ERM`

## Mechanism
EEG Conformer is a convolutional tokenizer (temporal then spatial convolution producing patch tokens) feeding a transformer self-attention encoder and an MLP head, on EA-aligned trials. A largely attention-based backbone; it is data-hungry and carries the largest across-seed variance in the sweep on this small dataset — a faithful below-baseline result, not a bug.

Accuracy is the mean over seeds; ± is the standard deviation *across* seeds (a reproducibility figure, not the cross-subject spread). The reference range is the published / expected band on this dataset.
