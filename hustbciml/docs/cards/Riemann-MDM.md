# Riemann-MDM

**Axis:** Classical (network-free) &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Paradigm:** MI &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Dataset:** BNCI2014001 (cross-subject LOSO)

**Stage configuration:** `EA · covariance · (no backbone) · MDM · fit`

## Mechanism
Minimum Distance to Riemannian Mean represents each trial by its spatial covariance matrix and classifies by the smallest affine-invariant Riemannian distance to each class's geometric-mean covariance. Network-free and deterministic.
