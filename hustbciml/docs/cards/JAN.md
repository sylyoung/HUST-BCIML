# JAN

**Axis:** Transfer / adaptation strategy &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Paradigm:** MI &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Dataset:** BNCI2014001 (cross-subject LOSO)

**Stage configuration:** `EA · no-aug · EEGNet · Linear · JAN`

## Mechanism
Joint Adaptation Network extends the MMD idea by aligning the joint distribution across multiple layers (feature and softmax) with a joint Maximum Mean Discrepancy, rather than matching each layer's marginal separately. One of the tightest-variance strategies here.
