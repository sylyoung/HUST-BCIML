# ASFA

**Axis:** Transfer / adaptation strategy &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Paradigm:** MI &nbsp;·&nbsp; **Dataset:** BNCI2014001 (cross-subject LOSO)

**Stage configuration:** `EA · no-aug · EEGNet · Linear · ASFA`

## Mechanism
ASFA is source-free adaptation: after ERM source training the classifier head is frozen and only the feature extractor is adapted on the unlabeled target, by reducing a Tsallis-entropy prediction-uncertainty objective (temperature rescale, per-instance entropy reweighting, per-class normalization) together with a consistency regularizer from a DropMin auxiliary head. No source data is kept at adapt time.

| Metric | Value |

| Accuracy (mean ± std across seeds) | 73.28 ± 0.51 |

Accuracy is the mean over seeds; ± is the standard deviation *across* seeds (a reproducibility figure, not the cross-subject spread). The reference range is the published / expected band on this dataset.

- **Implementation:** Adapted from Xia et al. 2022 (IEEE TBME) onto the Strategy ABC. SGD, lr 0.01, 10 epochs; no third-party code beyond the ported objective.
